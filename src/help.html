System Help & User Guide

This guide explains how to use all features of the Ocean View Resort Reservation Management System.

1. System Overview

The system manages reservations, rooms, billing, revenue tracking, and secure user administration.

Roles: STAFF – Handles reservations and billing.
ADMIN – Manages users and rooms.

2. Login & Access

  • Enter your username and password.
  • Click Login.
  • You will be redirected based on your role.

3. Dashboard

  • Daily reservations
  • Check-in today
  • Check-out today
  • Occupied & available rooms
  • Daily & monthly revenue
  • Booking Activity
  • Revenue Trend (Last 7 Days)
  • Room Occupancy

4. Add Reservation

  • Enter guest details.
  • Select available room.
  • Choose check-in and check-out dates.
  • Click Confirm Reservation.

5. View Reservations

  • Search by reservation number.
  • View all reservations list.
  • Delete reservation if required.

6. Room Management

  • Rooms marked AVAILABLE or OCCUPIED.
  • Status updates automatically during booking & checkout.

7. Billing & Check-Out

  • Guest must be checked-out first.
  • Click Print Bill from reservation list.
  • Total nights and revenue are calculated automatically.

Revenue is recorded only when printing the bill.

8. Admin Functions

  • Create and manage users.
  • Add room types and rooms.
  • Update pricing.
  • Delete rooms or users if needed.

9. Common Issues

  • Invalid login – Check credentials.
  • No available rooms – All rooms occupied.
  • Check-out failed – Verify reservation status.
  • Access denied – Insufficient permissions.

10. Security Tips

  • Do not share credentials.
  • Always log out after use.
  • Admins should limit system changes.
